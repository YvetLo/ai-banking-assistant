# Loan Products Guide

## Home Mortgage Loan

### Interest Rate Options

| Plan | Rate Type | Rate Range | Description |
|------|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| Fixed Rate | Fixed | 2.8% – 3.5% p.a. | Stable monthly payments; ideal for risk-averse borrowers |
| Floating Rate | Variable | 2.2% – 3.0% p.a. | Adjusts with market rates; lower initial rate |
| Step Rate | Mixed | First 2 years: 1.5%, then variable | Lower initial burden; suitable for first-time buyers |

*Actual rates are determined by your credit profile, loan-to-value ratio, and other factors. The above are reference ranges, not guaranteed rates.*

### Loan Conditions

- **Maximum Loan-to-Value (LTV)**: Up to 80% for owner-occupied properties (subject to policy)
- **Loan Term**: Up to 30 years
- **Minimum Loan Amount**: NT$1,000,000
- **Repayment Methods**: Equal principal-and-interest (fixed monthly payment) or equal principal (decreasing monthly payment)

### Required Documents

1. National ID card
2. Last 3 months' salary transfer records or salary certificate
3. Land and building title deed (the bank can assist with retrieval)
4. Sale and purchase agreement (for buyers) or property registration certificate (for existing owners)
5. Last 2 years' income tax returns (required for self-employed applicants)

### Prepayment

- **Full Prepayment within 2 Years**: Early termination fee of 2% of outstanding balance
- **Partial Prepayment**: Allowed at any time with no penalty; reduces principal
- **Full Prepayment after 2 Years**: No early termination fee

## Personal Credit Loan (Unsecured Loan)

### Product Features

- **No Collateral Required**: Approved based on personal creditworthiness
- **Fast Approval**: As quickly as 1 business day
- **Flexible Repayment**: Choose from 12 to 84 months

### Rates & Limits

| Credit Rating | Reference Rate (p.a.) | Maximum Loan Amount |
|--------------|----------------------|-------------------|
| Excellent | 3.88% – 5.99% | NT$2,000,000 |
| Good | 6.00% – 9.99% | NT$1,000,000 |
| Standard | 10.00% – 14.99% | NT$500,000 |

*Actual rates are determined by your credit score, income, and existing debt levels.*

### Eligibility

- Age 20 or above with full legal capacity
- Stable monthly income of NT$25,000 or more
- No recent serious delinquency on credit record

### How to Apply

- **Online**: Via Online Banking or Mobile App → Loan Center → Personal Loan
- **Branch**: Visit any branch with your ID and income verification
- **Phone**: Call 0800-XXX-XXX; a specialist will assist you

## Mortgage Payment Calculator (Reference)

**Monthly Payment Formula (Equal Principal-and-Interest)**:

```
Monthly Payment = Loan Amount × [Monthly Rate × (1 + Monthly Rate)^N] ÷ [(1 + Monthly Rate)^N - 1]
Monthly Rate = Annual Rate ÷ 12
```

**Example Calculation**:
- Loan Amount: NT$8,000,000
- Annual Rate: 2.5% (Monthly Rate ≈ 0.2083%)
- Term: 360 months (30 years)
- Estimated Monthly Payment: **approximately NT$31,617**

*This is an estimate only. Use the mortgage calculator on our website for precise figures.*

## FAQ

**Q: How long does mortgage approval take?**
A: With complete documentation, approval typically takes 5–7 business days. Funds are disbursed 1–2 business days after property title transfer.

**Q: Can I apply for a loan term extension?**
A: Yes. Submit a request for review. The interest rate may be adjusted upon extension. Please contact your loan specialist for details.

**Q: Can I apply for both a mortgage and a personal loan simultaneously?**
A: Yes, but the bank will assess your total debt-to-income ratio. Monthly repayments across all loans generally should not exceed 50% of your monthly income.
