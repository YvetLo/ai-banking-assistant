# ATM Services Guide

## Cash Withdrawals

### Daily Withdrawal Limits

| Account Type | Daily Withdrawal Limit |
|-------------|----------------------|
| Standard Account | NT$20,000 |
| After Limit Increase Request | NT$30,000 |
| Temporary Increase (Branch Application) | Up to NT$100,000 |

- The daily limit resets at 00:00 each day
- To adjust your limit: visit a branch with your ID or apply via Online Banking

### Single Transaction Limit
- Maximum NT$20,000 per transaction
- Multiple consecutive withdrawals are allowed up to the daily limit

## Interbank ATM Fees

| Time | XX Bank ATM | Other Bank ATM |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| Weekdays 09:00–15:00 | Free | NT$15 / transaction |
| All other hours | Free | NT$17 / transaction |

- **Interbank ATM**: Any ATM not operated by XX Bank, including those at convenience stores
- Using your XX Bank card at 7-Eleven (ibon ATM) or FamilyMart (FamiPort): interbank fees apply as above

---

## Deposit Function

- **Cash Deposit**: Available at select ATMs; minimum NT$100, no maximum limit
- **Check Deposit**: Available at check-enabled ATMs or at a branch counter
- Cash deposits are posted immediately (same day); checks require clearing

---

## Transfer Function

- Same bank transfers: Free, instant
- Interbank transfers: NT$15 per transaction, instant
- Daily transfer limit at ATM: NT$30,000 (lower than Online Banking for security)

---

## ATM Card Information

### Chip Debit Card
- Supports VISA / Mastercard international withdrawals (overseas ATMs)
- Overseas withdrawal fee: NT$75 per transaction + 1.5% currency conversion fee
- Overseas daily withdrawal limit: equivalent to NT$20,000

### Magnetic Stripe Debit Card
- For domestic use in Taiwan only
- We recommend upgrading to a chip debit card for enhanced security and overseas access

### Card Security Settings
- Set daily withdrawal limits and enable/disable international withdrawals via Online Banking
- Three consecutive incorrect PIN entries: card is automatically locked; visit a branch with your ID to unlock

## FAQ

**Q: The ATM swallowed my card. What do I do?**
A: If it's an XX Bank ATM, press the "Call Service" button or call 0800-XXX-XXX. If it's another bank's ATM, contact that bank directly.

**Q: I forgot my ATM PIN. Can I reset it at an ATM?**
A: No. You must visit a branch in person with your ID to reset your PIN. The process takes approximately 10 minutes.

**Q: Can I pay bills at a convenience store ATM with my debit card?**
A: Yes. You can pay bills and utility fees at 7-Eleven (ibon) or FamilyMart (FamiPort) kiosks using your XX Bank chip debit card.

**Q: How quickly does a cash deposit appear in my account?**
A: Cash deposits are credited immediately (by 23:59 the same day) and can be used right away.
