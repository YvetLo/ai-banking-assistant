# Credit Card Fees Guide

## Annual Fees

XX Bank credit card annual fees vary by card type:

| Card Type | Annual Fee | Fee Waiver Conditions |
|-----------|-----------|----------------------|
| Standard Card | NT$800 | Waived with 3+ transactions per year |
| Gold Card | NT$1,500 | Auto-waived with NT$30,000+ annual spending |
| Platinum Card | NT$3,500 | First year free; permanently waived with NT$150,000+ annual spending |
| Infinite Card | NT$18,000 | First year free (subject to approval); permanently waived with NT$600,000+ annual spending |

To apply for an annual fee waiver, call the number on the back of your card, log in to Online Banking, or visit any branch.

---

## Revolving Interest

If you do not pay the full balance by the due date, revolving interest will apply:
- **Annual Interest Rate**: 6.85% – 15% (determined by your credit profile)
- **Interest Calculation**: Charged from the transaction posting date until full repayment
- We recommend paying the full balance each month to avoid interest charges

---

## Cash Advance

- **Transaction Fee**: 3% of the advance amount, minimum NT$100
- **Interest Rate**: 15% per annum, charged from the day of the advance
- **Advance Limit**: Up to 50% of your credit limit, maximum NT$150,000
- Cash advances do not earn cashback rewards or bonus points

---

## Foreign Transaction Fees

- **Currency Conversion Fee**: 1.5% of the transaction amount
- **Overseas Transaction Fee**: 1.5% of the transaction amount
- Combined total: approximately 3%, shown on your monthly statement

---

| 6 months | 0.49% |

---

## Card Replacement Fees

- **Reporting Lost/Stolen**: Free
- **Replacement Card**: NT$200 (magnetic stripe); NT$300 (chip card)
- If the replacement is due to a bank system issue, the fee is waived

## FAQ

**Q: How do I check my credit card's annual fee due date?**
A: Log in to Online Banking → Credit Card Management → Annual Fee Information.

**Q: The annual fee has been charged. Can I get a refund?**
A: If you meet the waiver conditions but have already been charged, apply for a refund within 30 days of the charge by contacting customer service.

**Q: What is my revolving interest rate?**
A: Your individual rate is based on your credit score. Log in to Online Banking → Credit Card Details to view your applicable rate.
