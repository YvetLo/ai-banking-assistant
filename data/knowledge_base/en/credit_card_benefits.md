## Checking & Redeeming Rewards

**How to Check**:
- Online Banking → Credit Cards → Rewards/Points Summary
- Mobile App → Card Management → My Points

**How to Redeem**:
- Statement credit: Apply via Online Banking; deducted from next bill
- Gift redemption: Visit the Rewards Catalog on our website
- Miles redemption: Link your airline frequent flyer account first

## FAQ

**Q: Why haven't my rewards appeared yet?**
A: Points are credited by the 10th of the following month after your purchase posts. If they haven't appeared after 15 days, please contact customer service.

**Q: Can I use my points to offset someone else's bill?**
A: No. Points can only be applied to the primary cardholder's own account.

**Q: My points are about to expire. What should I do?**
A: Log in to Online Banking to check your expiry date, then redeem them through the Rewards Catalog before they expire.
