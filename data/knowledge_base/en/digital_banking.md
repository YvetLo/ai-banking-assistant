# Digital Banking Services Guide

## Online Banking

### Available Features

| Category | Features |
|----------|----------|
| Account Management | Balance inquiry, transaction history, e-passbook |
| Credit Card | Bill inquiry, payment, installment application, credit limit inquiry |
| Transfers | Same-bank and interbank transfers, registered payee setup |
| Deposits | Time deposit application/early withdrawal, interest rate inquiry |
| Loans | Repayment inquiry, prepayment application |
| Settings | Password change, transaction limit settings, notification preferences |

### Getting Started

1. Visit any branch with your ID to activate Online Banking access
2. Set up your Online Banking username and password
3. Optionally apply for a **Digital Certificate** to unlock advanced features (e.g., higher transfer limits)

### Login Methods

- Username and password
- Chip debit card + card reader (highest security level)
- Mobile OTP verification

## Forgot Your Password?

### Resetting Online Banking Password

**Option 1: Online Reset (requires registered mobile number)**
1. Go to the Online Banking login page → click "Forgot Password"
2. Enter your account number and National ID number
3. Receive a one-time OTP via SMS (sent to your registered mobile number)
4. Set a new password

**Option 2: Branch Reset**
- Visit any branch with your ID
- Password reset is completed on the spot

### Resetting ATM PIN
- Can only be done at a branch counter; online reset is not available
- Bring your ID to any branch; takes approximately 10 minutes

## Account Security Settings

### Recommended Security Features
- **Instant Spending Alerts**: Push notification for every transaction (free)
- **Unfamiliar Device Login Alert**: Notifies you when a new device logs in
- **Transaction Confirmation**: Two-step verification for large transfers (OTP)

### Responding to Suspicious Transactions
If you notice unauthorized activity, immediately:
1. Call our 24-hour hotline 0800-XXX-XXX to suspend your account
2. Or go to App → Security Center → Emergency Account Freeze
3. File a police report and obtain a report reference number
4. Bring the police report to a branch to complete the formal dispute process

## E-Statements

- **How to Apply**: Online Banking / App → Account Settings → E-Statement
- Paper statements are discontinued from the following billing cycle
- Statements are sent to your registered email and viewable in the App
- You can switch back to paper statements at any time

## FAQ

**Q: The app keeps showing "Device Not Authenticated." What should I do?**
A: Go to Online Banking → Security Settings → Device Management, remove the old device, and re-authenticate on your new phone. You can also contact customer service for assistance.

**Q: I'm not receiving the OTP text message. What should I do?**
A: Confirm that your registered mobile number is up to date. If you've changed your number, you must update it at a branch. Try toggling between Wi-Fi and mobile data before requesting the OTP again.

**Q: My Online Banking transfers are locked. What should I do?**
A: Multiple incorrect transaction password attempts may temporarily lock your transfer function. Call 0800-XXX-XXX to unlock, or it may automatically reset at 00:00 depending on the lock type.
