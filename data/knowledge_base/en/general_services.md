## Complaints & Disputes

If you are dissatisfied with our service, you may:

1. **Direct Feedback**: Speak with the branch manager or contact customer service
2. **Written Complaint**: Submit a feedback form on our website; response within 5 business days
3. **Financial Ombudsman Institution (FOI)**: If unsatisfied with our resolution, you may apply for independent mediation (free of charge)
   - Website: www.foi.org.tw
   - Phone: (02) 2506-6600

## FAQ

**Q: How do I update my registered address?**
A: Visit any branch, or log in to Online Banking → Personal Information → Address Update (upload front and back of your ID).

**Q: Can I make an appointment at a branch?**
A: Yes, for selected services. Visit our website → Branch Services → Make an Appointment to choose a branch and time slot.

**Q: I want to apply for a loan. Which branch should I go to?**
A: Any branch can handle loan applications. We recommend calling ahead to schedule an appointment and mention the loan type so the branch can arrange a specialist to assist you.

**Q: I received a suspicious call claiming to be from XX Bank. How do I verify?**
A: Hang up immediately and call our official hotline 0800-XXX-XXX to verify. XX Bank will never ask you to transfer money via ATM or Online Banking for tax refunds or installment cancellations. If you receive such a request, hang up and report it to the police.
