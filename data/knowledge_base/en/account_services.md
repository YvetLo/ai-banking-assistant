# Account Services Guide

## Opening an Account

### Required Documents
- **Nationals**: National ID card + secondary ID (Health Insurance Card or Driver's License)
- **Foreign Nationals**: Passport + Alien Resident Certificate (ARC)
- **Minors (under 18)**: Minor's ID + legal guardian's ID + household registration document

### Account Opening Process
1. Visit any branch with the required documents
2. Complete the account application form
3. Make an initial deposit (minimum NT$1,000 for savings account)
4. Receive your passbook and ATM card (approximately 3–5 business days)

### Online Account Opening (Digital Account)
- Available for Taiwan nationals aged 18 and above
- Required: National ID card + Health Insurance Card + registered mobile phone
- Account activated immediately upon verification; ATM card mailed within 5–7 business days

## Account Types & Interest Rates

### Demand Deposit (Savings Account)
- Interest Rate: 0.2% per annum (subject to market rates; see website for current rates)
- Minimum Opening Deposit: NT$1,000
- No minimum balance requirement; deposits and withdrawals at any time

### Time Deposit (Fixed Deposit)
| Term | Annual Interest Rate |
|------|---------------------|
| 1 month | 0.85% |
| 3 months | 1.15% |
| 6 months | 1.35% |
| 1 year | 1.75% |
| 2 years | 1.85% |
| 3 years | 1.90% |

- Minimum deposit: NT$10,000
- Early withdrawal: Interest recalculated at the demand deposit rate based on actual days held

### Foreign Currency Deposits
- Available currencies: USD, EUR, JPY, HKD, AUD, CNY
- Interest rates: Based on current market rates for each currency; see our website for latest rates
- Requires a foreign currency account (can be opened simultaneously, no additional fee)

## Account Maintenance Fee

- **Regular Account**: Free if monthly average balance ≥ NT$3,000; NT$50/month if below
- **Digital Account**: No maintenance fee
- **Minor's Account**: No maintenance fee until age 18

---

## Passbook Replacement

- **Reported Lost**: Apply at a branch, free of charge
- **Lost Passbook Replacement**: Apply at a branch, processing fee NT$100
- **Full Passbook Exchange**: Apply at a branch, free of charge

## FAQ

**Q: How do I check my account balance?**
A: Check via Online Banking, Mobile App, ATM, or call our telephone banking service at 0800-XXX-XXX.

**Q: Will my time deposit auto-renew when it matures?**
A: Yes, it auto-renews by default for the same term. To stop renewal, visit a branch or log in to Online Banking before the maturity date.

**Q: How much will I lose if I withdraw my time deposit early?**
A: Early withdrawal is recalculated at the demand deposit rate. For example, if you hold a 1-year deposit at 1.75% but withdraw after 6 months, interest is recalculated at 0.2% for those 6 months.

**Q: Can I open a time deposit online?**
A: Yes. Log in to Online Banking → Deposit Services → Time Deposit → New Application.
