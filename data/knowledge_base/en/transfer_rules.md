# Transfer & Remittance Guide

## Domestic Transfers

### Transfer Methods & Fees

| Method | Same Bank | Interbank |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Online Banking | Free | NT$15 / transaction |
| Mobile App | Free | NT$15 / transaction |
| ATM | Free | NT$15 / transaction |
| Branch Counter | Free | NT$30 / transaction |
| Phone Banking | Free | NT$15 / transaction |

### Transfer Limits

| User Type | Single Transaction Limit | Daily Limit |
|-----------|------------------------|------------|
| Standard User | NT$1,000,000 | NT$3,000,000 |
| Mobile Phone Verified | NT$3,000,000 | NT$5,000,000 |
| Counter-applied Higher Limit | Subject to approval | Subject to approval |

### Real-Time Transfers (24/7)
- Same bank to same bank: Instant, available 24/7
- Interbank real-time transfer (FISC): Instant, NT$15 fee
- Non-real-time transfer: Credited by 09:00 the next business day

## Pre-authorized Account Transfers

Register a payee account in advance to access higher limits:
- **How to register**: At a branch counter or via Online Banking (digital certificate required)
- **Registered account limit**: NT$5,000,000 per transaction, NT$10,000,000 per day
- **Effective date**: Immediate for counter registration; next business day for online registration

---

## International Wire Transfers

### Outgoing Transfers (Remittance)
- **Service Fee**: NT$800 per transaction (cable fee of approx. USD$10 also applies)
- **Exchange Rate**: Bank's quoted rate on the day of transfer
- **Estimated Arrival**: USA/Europe: 2–3 business days; Asia: 1–2 business days
- **Required Information**: Beneficiary name, bank name, SWIFT Code, account number, beneficiary address

### Incoming Transfers
- **Receiving Fee**: NT$300 per transaction
- Upon receipt, you may retain the funds in foreign currency or convert to NTD

## Important Reminders

1. **Verify Account Numbers**: Double-check all account details before transferring. Completed transfers generally cannot be reversed.
2. **Fraud Prevention**: XX Bank will never ask you to transfer money via ATM or Online Banking to "refund taxes" or "cancel installments."
3. **Failed Transfers**: If the recipient's account is frozen or the details are incorrect, funds will be returned within 1 business day.
4. **Transfer History**: View up to 6 months of transfer records via Online Banking.

## FAQ

**Q: I transferred to the wrong account. What should I do?**
A: Contact our customer service immediately at 0800-XXX-XXX. We will request the recipient's bank to return the funds, but this cannot be guaranteed. Always double-check account details before transferring.

**Q: Why was my transfer suspended by the system?**
A: For security, the system may temporarily suspend transactions after multiple incorrect password entries or suspicious login activity. Please call customer service to lift the suspension.

**Q: Can I temporarily increase my daily transfer limit?**
A: Yes. Visit any branch with your ID to apply for a temporary limit increase, or apply via Online Banking if you hold a digital certificate.

**Q: How long does an interbank transfer take?**
A: Real-time interbank transfers typically arrive within 30 seconds to 2 minutes. Non-real-time transfers are credited by 09:00 the next business day.
